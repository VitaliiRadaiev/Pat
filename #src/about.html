About pat

Gateway for developing your logistics business effectively in Thailand with Port Authority of Thailand

Port Authority of Thailand (PAT) is a state enterprise under the Ministry of Transport.

Was established in accordance with the Port Authority of Thailand Act, B.E. 2494 with the objective to arrange operations and bring prosperity to the port business For the benefit of the state and the people.

At present, the Port Authority of Thailand is responsible for the management of the major ports such as Bangkok Port, Laem Chabang Port, Chiang Saen Commercial Port, Chiang Khong Port, And Ranong port.

Our Vision

To be the world's leading port along with providing excellent logistics services to reach sustainable
growth in 2030

Our Value

Adhere to good governance, service with heart, be creative, care for environment
